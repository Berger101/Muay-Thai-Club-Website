This is the home page of the Muay Thai club. Here you can find information about our activities, training sessions, and more.

Briefly about us

With us, you can practice Thai boxing, classic boxing and BJJ. And everyone can train - regardless of who you are, how old you are, and whether you're trying martial arts for the first time or have been to a lot of matches - with us everyone has a place and is equally welcome! We also have private training, group training for companies, associations and schools, and special events such as staff parties, kick offs, hen/stag parties and more.
